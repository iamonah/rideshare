Our proto field names stay in snake_case, because that is standard protobuf style. When those messages are 
exposed as JSON, protobuf maps them to camelCase by default. We should treat those camelCase names as the 
canonical client-facing field names, and keep our Go json tags, 
gateway payloads, and validation error field paths aligned with them.
